Painting the ultramarines infantry is one of the easier to do.at least in my humble opinion.
The key is grey seer contrast primer, which gives the models a cold blue base to begin with. This works nicely with the Talassar Blue contrast, which is the main and only source for the blue armour.
Weapons and backpack vents I paint black. The metallic parts I then paint in Vallejo metal series exhaust manifold, including the backpack parts.
The main spot colour for the infantry is white. I use this to pick out vets and sergeants sparingly.
To create a strong base, I use Ulthuan grey, my favourite go-to for white. Again, it works really well over blue. Highlight with pure white.
