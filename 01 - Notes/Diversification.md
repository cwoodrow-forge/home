Diversification is spreading your investments across a diversified portfolio of stocks. This could be by country (Global, US, EU), sector (tech, energy), emerging versus developed markets, spread. Diversification follows principle that the market as a 'whole' goes up on average. It does not make returns like betting on individual company performance, but a portfolio that spreads the risk to smooth out individual short term shocks (like Tesla suddenly dropping).

### **Key Takeaways for Diversification**

- **Broad market ETFs** (SPY, VTI) give exposure to many stocks.
    
- **International ETFs** (EEM, VEA) help diversify beyond U.S. markets.
    
- **Sector ETFs** (QQQ, XLE, XLV) allow focus on specific industries.
    
- **Dividend ETFs** (VIG, SCHD) generate income.
    
- **Bond ETFs** (AGG, BSV) provide stability.
    
- **Alternative ETFs** (ARKK, GLD) add speculative or defensive assets.
