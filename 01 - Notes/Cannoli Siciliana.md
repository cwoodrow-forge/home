## Description
Jamie's interpretation of the classic #italian dessert as taught by Gennaro. 
## Method
Blitz ricotta and 3 Tbsp sugar in a food processor for 1 minute until it forms a smooth glossy cream. Taste for sweetness and add more sugar if needed. Transfer the sweet ricotta to a bowl and add the chocolate, pistachios and glace fruit. Feel free to improvise.
Pipe the cannoli biscuits all the way through. Dust with icing sugar.
### Alternatives
Dip half a cannoli in melted chocolate and then into chopped nuts before filling.
Try with raspberries.
## Ingredients
- 6 Tbsp vanilla sugar
- 200g Ricotta
- 100g plain chocolate, chopped
- 50g pistachios, chopped
- 50g glace fruit, chopped (optional)
- 12 cannoli biscuits
- icing sugar
