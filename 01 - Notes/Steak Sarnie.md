## Description
One of many using Rump, but could be any cut.
## Method
Preheat oven to 100 / Gas 1. Cook steak to taste with rosemary and rest. Warm Ciabatta in oven until soft. Drizzle cut Ciabatta with extra virgin olive oil, smesr with Dijon and add steak slices and rocket. Drizzle over resting juices.

Alternative method: Cut steak in half and bash out under clingfilm until 1cm thick, cover with rosemary leaves. Flash it on griddle 1 minute until pink, then rest with lemon juice squeezed over it. 
## Ingredients
- 1 Ciabatta
- 10oz Rump Steak
- 1 sprigs rosemary, leaves picked
- 1 - 2 Tbsp Dijon
- 1 Lemon
- Handful rocket
- Extra Virgin olive oil
