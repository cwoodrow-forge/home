## Description
Unusual #pasta dish. Can be swapped for Penne or Rigatoni.
## Method
Trim the brocolli to remove the florets as small as possible. Finely chop the stalks and add to pan with garlic, anchovies, chillies and half the butter. Cover with lid and cook slowly for 8 - 10 minutes. Meanwhile, cook the pasta. 
Add the florets to the pasta for the last 3 - 4 minutes. Strain the pasta and florets and add to the pan with the sauce, remove from heat and toss together. Season to taste and add remaining butter, parmesan and a little of the cooking water to create a glossy sauce.
Serve with the remaining grated parmesan.
## Ingredients
- 1 Large head of brocolli
- 1 clove garlic, chopped
- 4 anchovy fillets
- dried chilli flakes
- 2 knobs butter
- Orecchiette
- grated parmesan
