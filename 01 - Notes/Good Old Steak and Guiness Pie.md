## Description
Jamie's meat pie with a puff pastry lid. Simple. Could probably do in slow cooker and then just re-heat and pop a pastry lid on it!
## Method
Season Beef generously, sprinkle with flour. Heat olive oil and brown meat. Add the onion (1 minute), then carrot, celery, parsnip and herbs. Fry for another 4 mins. Pour in Guiness, add tinned tomatoes, bring to boil. Simmer for at least 2 hours.
This works nice as a stew, but also matures in fridge really well.
#### For the pie
Preheat over to 190 / gas 5. Fill pie dishes with the stew and brush the rims with beaten egg. Cover with pastry and press down on edges to seal. Brush lid with more beaten egg.

Bake for 45 mins.

Serve with boiled potatoes or mash and greens. 
## Ingredients
- 680g stewing beef, diced
- 2 Tbsp plain flour
- olive oil
- 1 onion
- 1 carrot
- 4 sticks celery
- 2 parsnips
- Rosemary, thyme, bay
- 565ml Guiness
- 2 x tins tomatoes
- Ready roll puff pastry
- 1 egg, beaten
