## Taxes and their rates

### Capital Gains (CGT)
The Capital Gains Tax rate you use depends on the total amount of your taxable income, you should work that out first.
#### 2025/2026
- 18% and 24% for basic rate and higher rate tax payers
- £3000 personal allowance
