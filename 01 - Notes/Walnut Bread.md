## Description
Half-and-half wholemeal and white bread which will have a more closed structure and probably chewy. Might toast well. Go with honey I'm guessing.
## Method
Crush half the walnuts into small pieces and the rest into a fine powder. Make the dough including the nuts and apricots then allow the bulk prove. Shape into battons for second prove, laying them next to each other to make a single loaf. Allow second prove to double, then dust with flour, score and bake at 180 / gas 4 for around 30 minutes. Go in hotter if needed to get oven spring then turn down to 180.
Great serves with cheese and branston pickle. A Ploughmans candidate?
## Ingredients
- [[basic bread mix]] made with 500g wholemeal and 500g strong white
- 455g peeled walnuts
- 115g dried apricots, very finely chopped
- 115g butter, soft
