## Description
Surprise! whole orange in the middle of a steamed chocolate pudding.
## Method
Boil water in pan where going to steam pudding later. Put whole orange in for at least 10 minutes with lid on.
Grease a 1.3 ltr / 2 pint pudding basin with butter. Melt butter with chocolate in a bowl over the hot water. Put the flour and cocoa into a mixing bowl; add the melted butter and chocolate, then sugar, eggs and milk and mix well. 
Put around 2/3 mix into the pudding basin, making a well in the middle.
Prick the orange all over and place it in the middle of the mix. Dice the butter and dot it around the orange with the sugar. Cover with the remaining mix and cover the pudding with greaseproof paper, then tin foil. Tie and place in the pan of hot water (it should come half-way up the sides). Steam for 2 hours. Periodically check water to see if it needs topping up.
Turn onto a serving dish and serve with custard or cream.
## Ingredients
- 85g butter
- 55g dark chocolate
- 170g self-raising flour
- 55g cocoa powder
- 170g caster sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons milk
### filling
- 1 orange (thin-skinned navel variety if possible)
- 85g butter
- 85g caster sugar
