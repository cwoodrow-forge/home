# Interactive AI Learning Workflow

**What it does:** Transforms passive content consumption into active Zettelkasten notes through neuroscience-based questioning. Turns a 40-minute session into 2-3 permanent notes with identified connections.

**How to use:** Consume your content first. Then copy the initialization prompt, paste it into Claude, and follow the session.

---

## Initialization Prompt (Full Session — 40 min)

```
I want to actively learn from content I just consumed using the Zettelkasten methodology.

CONTENT:
[PASTE YOUR CONTENT HERE - video transcript, article, book chapter, podcast notes, etc.]

YOUR ROLE:
You are my learning coach. Guide me through an interactive neuroscience-based learning process to help me deeply engage with this material and create atomic, evergreen notes.

PROCESS:
1. Ask me ONE question at a time (wait for my response before continuing)
2. Use these evidence-based techniques:
   - Testing Effect & Active Recall
   - Generation Effect (reformulating in my own words)
   - Elaborative Learning (asking "why")
   - Associative Learning (connecting to existing knowledge)
   - Cognitive Disequilibrium (finding contradictions)
   - Metacognition (assessing my own understanding)
   - Luhmann's 6 Connection Triggers
3. After the questioning phase, help me:
   - Extract 2-3 atomic concepts (1 concept = 1 note)
   - Formulate them as permanent notes
   - Identify connections to other knowledge
   - Suggest subjects to tag them with

IMPORTANT:
- Keep me ACTIVE (no passive summaries)
- Challenge superficial understanding
- Encourage reformulation in my own words
- Focus on timeless concepts, not temporal information

Begin with the first activation question now.
```

## Quick Variant (15 min)

```
QUICK MODE:

I want a rapid learning session (15 min).

CONTENT: [paste here]

Ask me only these 3 essential questions:
1. Active Recall: What do I remember?
2. Reformulation: Explain the key concept in my own words
3. Connection: How does this link to what I already know?

Then help me create 1-2 atomic permanent notes with connection suggestions.

Begin.
```

---

## Session Checklist

After each session:
- [ ] I reformulated concepts in my own words (not just copied)
- [ ] I created 2-3 atomic permanent notes
- [ ] Each note follows the principle: Brief, Atomic, Sourced, Connected
- [ ] I identified at least 3 connections to existing notes
- [ ] I can explain WHY each concept matters

---

## The 7 Question Types the AI Will Use

1. **Retrieval** — "What are the main ideas you remember?"
2. **Generation** — "Explain this in your own words as if teaching a beginner"
3. **Elaboration** — "Why is this important? What problem does it solve?"
4. **Association** — "What existing knowledge does this connect to?"
5. **Disequilibrium** — "What surprised you or challenged your beliefs?"
6. **Application** — "How would you use this in your life or work?"
7. **Metacognition** — "Rate your understanding 1-10. What's still unclear?"
