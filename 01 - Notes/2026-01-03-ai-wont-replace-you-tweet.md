# AI Won't Replace You Tweet

AI won't replace creators.

It will replace creators who don't have a unique perspective.

Because AI can produce any piece of content in seconds.

What it can't produce: your specific combination of experiences, failures, obsessions, and hard-won insights.

That network — if you build it — is your moat.

The question isn't "how do I use AI?"

It's "what are you building that AI can't replicate?"
