## Ingredients
- Sea Bass fillets
- San Mazano cherry tomatoes
- Capers
- Baby leaf spinach
- new potatoes
- lemon
- Garlic clove

## Method
Boil New potatoes. While boiling, saute sliced garlic and capers for a minute, add halved tomatoes and saute until soft. Pan fry the Sea Bass until skin crispy, ad spinach to tomatoes until wilted, then add squeze lemon. Serve with the sea bass and new potatoes.
