## Description
Goes with anything style onion baguettes. Good for just centre of table type bread. Useful having a baguette cooking tray!
## Method
Into a pot heat oil, garlic and thyme. Then add onions, cover and cook for 4 - 5 minutes. Remove lid  and continue cooking until transluscent and water evaporated. Do not let colour. Season and add splash vinegar.
Make [[basic bread mix]] Bulk prove then divide into 4 or 6. Roll into classic baguette shape. Move to a flour dusted baking rack, dust with flour, score and rub the onion mixture over the top of each. Allow to final prove and double in size. 
Bake for 15 minutes at 180 / gas 4. The burnt onions are part of it.

## Ingredients
- [[basic bread mix]]
- olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, sliced
- handful fresh thyme, leaves picked
- 3 onions, finely sliced
- splash white wine vinegar
