## Description
Another summer use for all that rosemary in an oily #fish dish. Simple and another use for breadcrumbs. Cheap dish. Although need to find a use for the fennel. Could be a #main also with a salad.
## Method
Preheat oven to 220 / gas 7
Finely chop the fennel bulb, retain leafy fronds. Whizz up ciabatta into breadcrumbs and add to bowl with fennel, pinenuts and marjoram. Season and mix in extra virgin olive oil to create a crumb.
Lay out 1 slice pancetta, place sardine fillet across it at right-angles, skin side down. Sprinkle the sardine with breadcrumb mix. Place another sardine on top, skin-side up and add a little more breadcrumb mix. Wrap the pancetta around it and fix with a sharpened rosemary sprig. Repeat until you have 8.
 Lay them on oiled greaseproof paper in a roasting tray and bake at top of oven for 8 minutes, or until bacon and crumbs are crispy.
 Serve with a little rocket salad and the fennel fronds. Wedge of lemon.
## Ingredients
- 1 small fennel bulb
- stale ciabatta
- handful pinenuts, toasted.
- fresh marjoram leaves
- extra virgin olive oil
- 8 slices panetta or dry-cured smoked streaky bacon
- 8 fresh sardines, scaled, gutted and filleted.
- 8 rosemary sprigs
