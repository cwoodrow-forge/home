## Description
A rich #asian style satay dressing that is particularly good on Udon Noodle salad dishes or slaw.
## Method
Blitz all ingredients in a #nutri-bullet until smooth. Note: warm water will need to be added to the ingredients before blitzing as this emulsifies into a very thick paste otherwise. You may need more water than you think! Adjust for consistency to create a dressing that can be drizzled onto noodles and salad.
Used in [[Udon Noodle Salad]] and [[Asian Slaw]]
## Ingredients
- 1 clove garlic
- Juice of 1 Lime
- 1 Tbsp Rice Vinegar
- 2 Tbsp Light Soy
- 1 Tbsp Gouchujang
- 1 Tbsp Honey
- 4 Tbsp Peanut Butter
- 1 Tsp Sesame Oil
- Warm water to loosen.
