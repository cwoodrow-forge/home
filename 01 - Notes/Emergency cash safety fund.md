## Emergency cash safety fund

An emergency cash safety fund is a cash reserve, kept in a high-interest savings account with sole purpose of insulating against an unexpected financial shock. This could be a market crash, or an unexpected large outlay for house repairs. This should be a savings account you don't expect to need to dip into, but can if needed.

Not same as easy access, where you may expect to dip into it regularly.
