## Description
Either a light main or generous #starter this #seafood dish is light and simple. .
## Method
Blanch asparagus and leeks for a couple of minutes and drain. Then cook in frying pan with olive oil in batches.  Season and cook until coloured on all sides and tender. Remove to a bowl and add half of torn up herbs and squeeze of lemon. Leave to one side while cooking scallops.
Score the scallops half-way through with a criss-cross on one side. Season on both sides with salt, pepper and five spice. Fry in hot pan 2 minutes until crust, then flip for 1 minute.
Divide leeks and asparagus between two warmed plates. Remove pan from heat, add 2 good knobs butter and juice from 1/2 lemon. Shake to mix, then lay scallops onto plate with juices and serve immediately.
## Ingredients
- 16 Asparagus spears
- 6 baby leeks
- extra virgin olive oil
- handful fresh marjoram or other summer herbs.
- 12 large scallops
- 1 lemon
- butter
- pinch five-spice
