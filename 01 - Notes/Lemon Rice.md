## Description
A jazzed up Basmati that's good with currys.
## Method
Make basmati as normal.
Heat oil in small frying pan, add mustard seeds until they pop, Ural Dal (small dried split peas), curry leaves and strips of lemon rind. Cook for 1 minute until lightly coloured. Add the rice to a bown and pour over the cooked spices, lemon juice and chopped coriander.

Serve with a good curry.
## Ingredients
- Basmati portion
- 2 Tbsp oil
- 1 Tbsp mustard seeds
- 1 Tbsp urad dhal
- Curry leaves
- Rind and juice of 1 lemon
- Fresh coriander, chopped.
