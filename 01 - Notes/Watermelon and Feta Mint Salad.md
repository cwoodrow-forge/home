## Description
The most refreshing thing you can eat in a heatwave. Sweet cold watermelon against briny feta, mint cutting through both. No cooking at all — assemble cold and dress at the table.

## Ingredients
- ½ small watermelon, cut into chunks or slices
- 200g feta, crumbled
- 1 small red onion, finely slivered
- Large handful fresh mint leaves
- Extra virgin olive oil
- Red wine vinegar or balsamic vinegar
- Black pepper
- Optional: ½ cucumber, sliced, for extra crunch

## Method
1. Cut watermelon into chunks or neat slices and arrange on a platter. Keep cold until the last moment.
2. Scatter over the crumbled feta, slivered red onion and mint.
3. Dress at the table with a drizzle of olive oil and a splash of red wine vinegar. Crack of black pepper.
4. Serve immediately — do not dress in advance.

## Notes
- Prep the watermelon and feta ahead and keep refrigerated, but assemble only just before eating
- Red onion can be soaked in cold water for 10 minutes to soften the sharpness
- Cucumber adds crunch if you want more substance
