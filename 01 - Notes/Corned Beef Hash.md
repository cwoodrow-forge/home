## Method
Lightly simmer the vegetables covered for 7 minutes until just cooked. Drain and set aside.
In frying pan, lightly fry onions until soft. Add the braised vegetables to pan with the corned beef. Cook for 5 minutes untouched until forms a crust. 
Add tomato puree and mustard to a jug with the hot beef stock and worcestershire sauce. Whisk well. 
Pour over beef and veg and stir well. Cook for another 10 minutes, stirring every so often. 
In a separate pan, fry the eggs.
Serve the hash with fried egg on top.

## Ingredients
- 200g Celeriac finely diced
- 200g Carrots finely diced
- 1 tsp fat or dripping scraped from the corned beef
- 1 onion finely chopped
- 1 tbsp tomato puree
- 1 tsp dijon mustard
- 200g can corned beef
- 100ml beef stock or consomme
- 1 tsp worcestershire sauce
- Eggs
