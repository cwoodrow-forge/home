## Description
Tray-baked #fish recipe that's good for cooking for many people without too much effort. Can be subbed for any flat fish like lemon sole. 
## Method
Preheat over to 200 / gas 6. Use small roasting tray so fish fits snugly. Put tray on hob and heat. Fry garlic in oil, before it colours add wine and cook for 1 minute. Turn heat down, add tomatoes and break down. Simmer.
Finely chop or blitz olives, basil and anchovy, then add oil to loosen. Think tapenade. Lay fillets on what was skin side and spread paste over the flesh side, Roll each up like a swiss roll and fix using a sharpened skewer of the rosemary. Place fish on top of the tomato sauce next to each other. Drizzle with olive oil and bake in oven for 15 minutes. Remove the fish to warm plates and place sauce back on hob for 1 minute. Throw in the spinach to just wilt. Spoon wilted spinach with sauce onto plates with the fish. Nothing else needed.

**Note:** Half the ingredients if cooking just for two.
## Ingredients
- extra virgin olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 glass chardonnay
- 2 x 400g tins plum tomatoes
- 150g black olives
- large handful fresh basil
- 2 anchovy fillets
- 4 x plaice fillets, skin removed.
- 4 sprigs rosemary
- Bag baby spinach, washed.
