## Description
A dressing style dip ideal for [[steaks]] with strong vinegar, garlic and herb notes. Similar to [[cowboy butter]] and [[compound butter]].
## Method
In a food processor, blitz all the ingredients except for the oil as it will emulsify.
Add oil at the end to create a dipping consistency that helps the sauce coat the tongue. This will keep for a few days. Probably great on [[burgers]] also.
## Ingredients
- Handful Parsley
- Handful Coriander (optional)
- Red Chilli flakes
- Garlic
- Oregano
- 2 Tbsp Red Wine Vinegar
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil
- Salt & Pepper
