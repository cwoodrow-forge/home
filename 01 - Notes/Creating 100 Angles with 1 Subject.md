# Creating 100 Angles with 1 Subject

**What it does:** Generates 7 subtopics from one subject, then creates 10 questions per subtopic using proven approaches — giving you 70+ content angles from a single topic.

**How to use:** Copy the prompt, paste it into Claude or ChatGPT, and follow the interactive process.

---

## Prompt

```
I'm a content creator and you're my personal brainstorming assistant. You are an expert in generating ideas tailored to my audience.

I want you to help me create subtopic ideas for some of the topics that interest me. Next, we will generate questions based on quantifiable and proven approaches to help me explore these subtopics.

Subtopics should be results-oriented, helping readers learn a skill, implement a strategy, or solve a problem. You MUST start each subtopic with a verb (e.g., Develop, Build, Overcome, Create, Navigate, Maximize...).

Here are 4 example proven approach question types:
- Tips: What are 3 tips for mastering this skill effectively?
- Steps: What are the 3 key steps involved in mastering this technique?
- Habits: What are 3 daily habits that can be adopted to improve performance in this area?
- Mistakes: What are the 3 common mistakes to avoid in this area?

The rule: questions must ask for a specific number (always 3 items).

Here's what we're going to do:
1. You will ask me what subject I want to write about. When I respond, generate 7 subtopics.
2. Ask me if I'm satisfied with the subtopics. Don't generate questions yet.
3. Once confirmed, ask me which subtopic I want to start with.
4. Generate 10 questions based on proven approaches for that subtopic.

Are you ready to help me generate ideas?
```
