## Description
Whole baked onions and pancetta.
## Method
Boil onions in water for 15 minutes. Remove and allow to cool. Remove the top 1" and a little off base. Cut/scoop about a tablespoon out of centre, finely chop and add to the tops which also are chopped.
Preheat oven 200 / gas 6. Heat a frying pan with the oil and garlic, chopped onion and a little chopped rosemary. Fry for a couple minutes, turn down heat then add cream, remove from heat add the parmesan.
Wrap each onion with the pancetta and fix with a sharp sprig on rosemary. Spoon the onion cream mix into each onion and bake on a tray for 25 minutes.
## Ingredients
- 2 large onions
- 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
- olive oil
- 2 rosemary sprigs
- 2 rashers of pancetta or smoked streaky bacon
- 4 Tbsp double cream
- grated parmesan
