## Description
Seared salmon fillets on a sweet and smoky salad leaves.
## Method
Char Radiccio on a searing griddle for a minute each side. Remove and place in a bowl. Cook pancetta until crisp, add to Radiccio. Add pinenuts and marjoran leaves to bowl. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic. and toss. Pile onto plates.
Sear salmon and serve with the salad.
## Ingredients
- 1 radiccio. Halved through the core and sliced
- Pancetta cubes or 8 rashers
- fresh marjoram (or basil)
- Handful toasted pinenuts
- extra virgin olive oil
- Balsamic
- 2 x 225g Salmon Fillet steaks. Skinned and pin boned.
