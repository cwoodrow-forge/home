## Description
Cost effective way of using up pancetta slices and bread as only need to buy Monkfish and in small quantity. Could freeze the rest of the #fish. Makes #kebabs
## Method
Preheat oven 220 / gas 7. Cut monkfish into 1" dice and add to bowl with ciabatta, ripped into similar sized pieces. Remove rosemary leaves leaving the top 5cm of each stalk. Place leaves into pestle with garlic and oil and bash up. Pour herby, garlicky oil over fish and bread and toss to mix.
Skewer the kebabs with fish and bread, repeating so there are at least three of each on each skewer finishing with fish at each end. wrap pancetta around skewers. Place on foil covered baking sheet, drizzle with remaining oil and rosemary and bake in over for 15 - 20 minutes. Drizzle with a little balsamic. Serve with a nice salad.
## Ingredients
- 220g monkfish tail (or any other white fish)
- Ciabatta, crust removed.
- fresh rosemary sprigs
- 1 glove garlic
- extra virgin olive oil
- 12 rashers pancetta or smoked streaky bacon
- balsamic vinegar
