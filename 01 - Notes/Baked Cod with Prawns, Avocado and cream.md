## Description
A curious one-pan over cooked cod with pawns and avocado and cream. Unusual.
## Method
Preheat oven to 220 / gas 7. Drizzle olive oil onto a baking pan. Season code on both sides and dd to the pan. Scatter over the basil, avocado and prawns. Drizzle over the cream and grated cheese. Cook in the top of the oven 15 - 20 minutes until golden and bubbling. Season and serve with a simple salad.
## Ingredients
- 2 x 225g Cod Fillets, skinless and pin-boned.
- Small handful torn Basil leaves
- 1 Avocado sliced
- 150g peeled prawns, cooked or uncooked.
- 140ml double cream
- 150g Grated cheddar
- Extra Virgin olive oil.
