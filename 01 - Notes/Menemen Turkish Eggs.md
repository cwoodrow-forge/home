## Description
Turkish style breakfast muddle of eggs, tomato and peppers with paprika and crumbled feta. Good way to use up left over peppers and feta from the [[cous cous and feta salad]]
## Method
Heat oil and fry onions and pepper in a covered pan for 10 minutes. Sprinkle over paroka and oregano, add tomato puree. Cook out for a minute or two. Add the chopped tomatoes.
Simmer for 5 minutes.
Stir in the eggs and continue stirring until full incorporated with tomato mix and have just set as a scramble.
Serve immediately with crumbled feta and chopped parsley or mint.

## Ingredients
- small red onion chopped
- 1 red pepper seeded and chopped
- 1 green pepper seeded and chopped
- 1 tsp hot paprika
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tbsp tomato puree
- can chopped toms or diced ripe tomatoes
- 6 eggs, beaten
- Feta
- Parsley or mint leaves
