## Description
Pan fried Salmon with crispy skin and a salad of creamed horseradish, rocket and baby beetroot.
## Method
Pan fry salmon skin side down. Weigh down with a protein press. After a minute, transfer to a baking tray, flip over, sprinkle with salt and place under grill for 10 - 15 minutes.
While cooking, marinade the sliced beetroots with marjoram, olive oil and balsamic with seasoning.
Mix the creme fraiche with the horseradish and season to taste with lemon juice and pepper.
Serve salmon with the rocket, beets and drizzled over horseradish.
## Ingredients
- 2 x 225g Salmon Fillets. Thick, skin on. Scored skin.
- 6 - 8 baby beetroots, cooked and sliced.
- marjoram leaves
- extra virgin olive oil
- balsamic vinegar
- 2 Tbsp creme fraiche
- 2 Tbsp creamed horseradish
- Juice 1 lemon
- Handful rocket
