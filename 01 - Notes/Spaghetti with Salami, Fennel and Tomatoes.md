## Description
Tuscany inspired #pasta that could also be great with cured sausage. Very hearty dish.
## Method
Fry the salami in olive oil with garlic and lightly cracked fennel seeds. 1 minute on low heat. Add sliced fennel, cover and cook for 5 minutes. Add tinned tomatoes, dried chilli flakes (optional). Cool on low for 25 minutes until thickened.
Cook the pasta.
#### Make the pangritata
Fry a handful of coarse breadcrumbs (whizzed up crusts) with a glug of extra virgin olive oil. Until crispy. Can also add chopped rosemary.
Serve pasta and sauce with the pangritata scattered over the top.
## Ingredients
- Spaghetti or similar
- 70g Spicy Italian salami, sliced
- extra virgin olive oil
- 1 Tsp fennel seeds
- 1 clove garlic, sliced
- 1 bulb fennel chopped. Fronds reserved
- 1 400g tin plum tomatoes
- Dried chilli flakes (optional)
- Stale breadcrumbs
- Rosemary leaves (optional)
