## Description
Sweet bread best served warm. Sounds very Middle Earth to me.
## Method
Puree bananas in nutri-bullet with enough water to make 1 pint (625ml). Make a [[basic bread mix]] swapping out the water for the banana mix. Also add half the honey with the nuts to the dough.
After bulk prove divide into 10 balls, shape and pack into a flour-dusted baking tin together for second prove. Think tray bake or sharing bread. 
Drizzle with the remaining honey and bake at 190 / gas 5 for 20  minutes.
Nice served warm with butter for breakfast.

## Ingredients
- Basic bread dough
- 6 bananas
- 8 tbsp runny honey
- handful chopped almonds, or flaked almonds, or any nuts really.
