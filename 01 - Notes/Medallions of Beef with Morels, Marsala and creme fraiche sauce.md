## Description
Indulgent dinner given cost of #beef medallions and morels, but quick to make.
## Method
Rehydrate the morels in hot water. Finely chop half the thyme and sprinkle over the medallions with a little olive oil. Rub into meat and rest.
Season steaks and sear for 2 minutes a side. remove and rest. 
Turn down the heat, add more olive oil and saute shallots, garlic and remaining thyme. After a minute, strain the morels and add to the pan. Add the marsala, flambe for a few seconds. Add creme fraiche along with any meat juices. Allow sauce to thicken.
Serve medallions with the sauce and mash, or bashed celeriac, or braised greens. Wilted spinach?
## Ingredients
- handful fresh thyme leaves
- 4 beef fillet medallions
- olive oil
- handful of dried morels
- 2 shallots, finely chopped
- 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
- 1 glass marsala
- 4 Tbsp creme fraiche
