## Description
Less of a dessert and more a garnish for a mousse or ice cream maybe.
## Method
Add sugar and 8 tablespoons water to a pan on medium heat. Use a spoon to stir together and will become a syrup. Carry on cooking until light golden then add sesame seeds until dark golden. Pour onto an oiled non-stick tray. Use palette to push about until as thin as possible (1/4" or even thinner). Allow to cool for 15 minutes. Bash up as you like into shards. Could probably even blitz into a crumb. Maybe clean out that Krups coffee grinder for that?
## Ingredients
- 455g caster sugar
- 200g sesame seeds
