## Description
More of a #pasta salad. Could also be a #side as it is a cold pasta dish.
## Method
Boil the pasta and garlic cloves. Drain, cool the pasta under cold water and put the garlic aside for the dressing. Add cooled pasta to a bowl. Chop tomatoes, olives, basil, chives and cucumber into small dice and add to bowl. Squeeze garlic out of skins and crush in mortar and pestle. Add the vinegar, oil and seasoning. Drizzle garlic dressing over the salad.
## Ingredients
- 155g small shell shaped pasta
- 1 cloves garlic. unpeeled.
- 255g mixed cherry tomatoes
- Black olives
- Fresh chives
- 1/4 cucumber
- basil
- 2 Tbsp white wine vinegar
- 4 Tbsp Extra Virgin olive oil
