## Method
Heat oil in saucepan. Add onion and pepper, fry for a few minutes until soft, add garlic and sweet potato.
Add 2 teaspoons chipotle paste and cumin. Stir to combine. Add chopped coriander stals and sweetcorn, pour in tomatoes and stock. season.
Bring to boil and simmer 10 minutes until sweet potato is tender.
While simmering, gentry fry bacon with chipotle paste. 
Blend half the soup smooth, then tip back into pan.
Serve with bacon and coriander leaves.

## Ingredients
- 2 tsp olive oil
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 1 red pepper, deseeded and diced
- 1 garlic clove, fiely chopped
- 1 sweet potato, finely diced
- 3 tsp chipotle paste
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- bunch coriander, stalks and leaves separated and chopped.
- 700g frozen sweetcorn (or tinned)
- 400g tin chopped tomatoes
- 1 ltr hot chicken or vegetable stock
- 75g smoked back bacon, trimmed and cut into strips
