# Your Second Brain Tweet

Most people use their note app as a storage system.

The best thinkers use it as a *thinking machine.*

The difference:

Storage: save idea → forget it → never use it

Thinking machine: save idea → connect it → generate new ideas from the collisions

One habit separates them:

Every new note must link to at least one existing note.

That's the whole system.
