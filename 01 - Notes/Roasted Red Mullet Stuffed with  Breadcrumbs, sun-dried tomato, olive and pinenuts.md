## Description
A roasted stuffed whole Red Mullet. Can also be left to go cold and used on a bruschetta as a #fish #starter once removed from bone. Add some leafy greens, oil and lemon and serve on toasted Ciabatta.
## Method
Preheat over 200 / gas 6. Remove crusts from ciabatta and whizz into breadcrumbs. Add to bowl with sun-dried tomatoes, olives, pinenuts and marjoram and mix, loosening with a couple lugs olive oil.
Season the fish inside and out and place on an oiled parchment over a baking tray. Stuff insides with the mix, pushing some into the scored slits in skin. Sprinkle remaining around the fish. Roast in top of oven for around 20 minutes until fish is cooked. Serve with a crisp salad. Sprinkle the excess crisped breadcrumbs over the salad. 
## Ingredients
- 1/2 ciabatta
- handful sun-dried tomatoes
- olives, chopped
- handful pinenuts
- fresh marjoram leaves
- olive oil
- 2 x 225g Whole Red Mullet, scaled, gutted and scored.
