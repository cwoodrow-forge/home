The following palette is my colour scheme for painting solar auxilia tanks for Legions Imperialis. I wanted these to represent the strong grey/green of the Terran Solar Auxilia, but have a distinct and strongly contrasting look of their own.

The grey starts with a black primer followed by a Tamiya Neutral Grey with airbrush. I lightened with a Tamiya Japanese Grey-Green, and finally a light blue-grey and white for highlights.

For the battalion or squadron markings I created bands or turret quarters of striking green usin g Games Workshop Contrast Warpstone Green.

Metal trim began as Victorian Brass and was highlighted in Auric Gold.

Metallics like tracks and turrets started as Vallejo Metal Series Exhaust manifold, highlighted in Duraluminium.
