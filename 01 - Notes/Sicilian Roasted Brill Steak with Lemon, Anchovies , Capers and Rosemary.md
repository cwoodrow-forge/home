## Description
Sicilian #fish recipe which would be even better if one could source Amalfi lemons!.
## Method
Preheat oven to 200 / gas 6. Bruise rosemary in a pestle and mortar. Add 3 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil. Add half the herby oil to the fix and place in an earthenware dish. Lay lemon slices over the top. Sprinkle over caper and drape over anchovies. Drizzle with remaining oil and bake in over for around 15 minutes (off the bone) or 25 minutes (bone in). Could also splash a little white wine in as well if liked. Remove and rest for 5 minutes.
Serve with steamed greens or a crispy salad.
## Ingredients
- Handful rosemary leaves
- extra virgin olive oil
- 2 x 200g brill, turbot or halibut steaks
- 1 large lemon, finely sliced using a mandolin)
- salted capers
- 4 anchovy fillets
- splash of white wine or prosecco
