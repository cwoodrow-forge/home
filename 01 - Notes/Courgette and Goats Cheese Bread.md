## Description
This is quite a flat bread, but really nice.
## Method
Make a basic dough, but cut the water down to 1/2 to accommodate for the grated courgette. Add more water if you think the dough is not hydrated enough. Bulk prove.
Add the goats cheese before final shaping into a round and second proving. Dust with flour, allow to double in size then score and bake in over at 180 / gas 4 for 30 minutes until crusty and hollow sounding.
Great toasted with some cream cheese or used as a normal sandwich bread.
## Ingredients
- [[basic bread mix]]
- 4 - 6 courgettes, grated and water squeezed out as much as possible.
- handful fresh thyme, leaves picked
- Goats cheese, crumbled.
