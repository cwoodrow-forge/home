## Description
An alternative pesto with balsamic.
## Method
Bash garlic in pestle and mortar, add basil and bash to a pulp. Bash in the pinenuts. Add the oil and vinegar. Mix and add seasoning to taste.
## Ingredients
- 1/2 garlic clove
- large handful basil
- small handful pinenuts
- 6 Tbsp Extra Virgin olive oil
- 3 Tbsp Balsamic vinegar
- Salt and pepper
