## Description
Parsnip and pancetta are a great combination together, think #risotto or even #soup. In this instance, they come together in a #pasta dish.
## Method
Fry pancetta and herbs in half the butter for 2 minutes, then add the garlic and parsnips. Cook for a further 3 minutes untik pancetta is golden and parsnips have softened. Cook Tagliatelle. Mix in the pasta, the rest of the butter, parmesan and some of the reserved pasta water to loosen and emulsify. Season to taste.
## Ingredients
- 6 slices pancetta or dry-cured smoked streaky bacon
- fresh rosemary or thyme leaves
- 2 knobs butter
- 1 clove garlic, finely sliced
- 1 parsnip, finely sliced lengthways
- tagliatelle
- grated parmesan
- Freshly grated black pepper
