## Description
Very quick and simple desert that's a good way to use up yoghurt or blueberry. Also a palette cleanser.
## Method
Divide yoghurt between glasses. Spoon over the blueberry or blueberry jam. Cover with elderflower cordial and top with mint tips.
## Ingredients
- 250g greek yoghurt
- 2 Tbsp blueberry jam
- 4 Tbsp elderflower cordial
- Fresh mint sprig tips
