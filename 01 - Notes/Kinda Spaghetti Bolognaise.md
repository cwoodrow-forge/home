## Description
Jamie's interpretation of #pasta Ragu.
## Method
Pre-heat oven to 180 / gas 4. In a pan that can go into oven, fry the pancetta and rosemary in a little olive oil until lightly golden. Add the onion and garlic, fry for another 3 minutes. Add the minced beef, continue for 2 - 3 more minutes. Add the wine, reduce slightly, add oregano, tomatoes and tomato puree. Bring to boil, cover in greaseproof, or a lid and place in over for an hour and a half.
Serve with spaghetti (or any other pasta really) with torn basil and grated parmesan.
## Ingredients
- 10 slices pancetta or smoked streaky bacon.
- rosemary leaves
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- 455g beef mice or chuck steak (minced)
- 1 glass red wine
- Tsp dried oregano
- 1 400g tin tomatoes
- tomato puree (or sun dried tomatoes)
- basil
- parmesan
- olive oil
