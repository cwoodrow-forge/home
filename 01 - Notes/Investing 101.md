The basics of investing is [[Diversification]]; basically not having all your investments dependent on the performance of one company stock, sector, country or fund. This is the principle of an Index Fund or [[ETF]] (Exchange Traded Fund).

A popular platform for ETFs is: https://www.justetf.com/en/
