## Description
Roasted Monkfish with sun-dried tomatoes and balsamic served on a buttery mash. Rick but simple #fish main.
## Method
Preheat oven 200 / gas 6. Blitz sun-dried tomatoes and their oil with basil until a paste. Add a drizzle of balsamic also (optional). lightly oil greasproof paper, add parma ham next to each other. Spread the tomato and basil paste, then add the monkfish fillets at one end, roll up. Remove paper and roast in oiled baking tray for 15 - 20 minutes.
Slice and serve with a really buttery mashed potato, rocket and a drizzle of balsamic.
## Ingredients
- jar sun-dried tomatoes in oil
- handful fresh basil
- olive oil
- 6 slices parma ham
- 2 x 200g monkfish tail fillets
- rocket
- balsamic (optional)
