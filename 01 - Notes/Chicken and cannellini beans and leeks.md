## Description
Chicken cooked in a foil parcel with beans, leeks, white wine and cream. See also: [[Chicken baked in foil with Mushrooms]]
## Method
Make a foil envenlope. Heat over to 220 / gas 7. Mix everything in a bown and add to bad, sealing the end. Slide onto a baking sheet and heat over a hob for 1 minute to get cooking going. Cook in oven for 25 minutes. Serve at table.

Could be nice with crusty bread.
## Ingredients
- 2 x chicken breasts
- 10 baby leeks (trimmed and washed)
- 1 x 400g tin cannellini beans, drained and washed. Squish the beans a bit.
- 1 clove garlic, sliced
- small handful fresh marjoram or oregano
- small glass white wine
- 140ml double cream
