## Description
Simple to make, but a dish probably for more than one serving. Not sure what it would be like cold next day? An #american version of our crumble.
## Method
Pre-heat oven 190 / gas 5. Add all the fruit to a pan with the sugar and balsamic and cook gently until juices begin to run from the cherries. Pour into ovenproof dish.
Rub the cold butter and flour to a fine breadcrumb. Add sugar, salt, stir then add the butter-milk to form a loose scone-like mixture. Spoon over the fruit to create a cobble-like texture. Sprinkle with caster sugar and bake for 30 minutes until golden brown.
Serve hot with vanilla ice cream.

## Ingredients
- 2 apricot, stoned and sliced
- 1 pear, cored and thickly sliced
- 1 punnet blackberries
- 1 punnet blueberries
- 1 punnet raspberries
- 1/2 apple, grated
- 5 Tbsp sugar
- glug balsamic vinegar
#### Topping
- 6 heaped Tbsp butter, chilled
- 225g self-raising flour
- 70g sugar
- large pinch salt
- 130ml butter-milk
