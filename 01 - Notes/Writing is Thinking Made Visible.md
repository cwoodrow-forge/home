# Writing is Thinking Made Visible

## Big Idea
You don't write because you have something to say — you write to figure out what you think. The act of writing forces vague intuitions into clear structures. An idea that "feels right" in your head often falls apart the moment you try to articulate it.

### Trigger Questions
- How does this note fit into what I already know (in my other notes)?
- How can this note explain another concept already in my system?
- What idea contradicts this idea?
- Why am I attracted to this idea (apart from confirmation bias)?
- If we change the author's perspective, does this note evolve?
- What are the applications I will have of this concept?
