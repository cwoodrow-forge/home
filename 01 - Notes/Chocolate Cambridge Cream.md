## Description
Similar to a creme brulee. Adaptable.
## Method
Scrape out vanilla seeds and chop pods. Put seeds and pods in heavy-base pan with the milk and cream and simmer slowly for 5 minutes. In a bowl that will fit over the pan, whisk the egg yolks, sugar and cocoa powder for a minute. While still whisking slowly add the infused milk and keep whisking. Add hot water to the dirty pan and bring to a simmer. Place the bowl over it and cook the custard slowly for 5 minutes stirring often until it coats back of a spoon.
Strain the custard through a seive to remove the vanilla pods. These can be discarded.
Pre-heat oven to 150 / gas 2. 
Place oven-proof dishes in high-sided roasting tin and divide chocolate between them. Shake flat. Carefully divide the custard amongst the dishes being careful not to disturb the chocolate.
Fill the tray with water until half way up the dishes. Cook for 30 - 45 minutes until slightly wobbly (not fully set) in the centre. Allow to cool overnight at room temp, or in fridge. Sprinkle with sugar, blow torch to caramelise.
## Ingredients
- 3 vanilla pods
- 200ml milk
- 375ml double cream
- 8 large egg yolks
- 70g caster sugar
- 1 heaped teaspoon cocoa powder
- 100g chocolate, bashed up finely
