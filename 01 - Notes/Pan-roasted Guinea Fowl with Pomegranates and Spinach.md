## Description
This is for Guinea Fowl or Pheasant, but Chicken will do fine in this #poultry dish. Good way to use up pomegranate.
## Method
Preheat oven 220 / Gas 7. In a bowl beat together ricotta and thyme. Season lightly. Carefully peel back skin on thighs and and drumsticks using two fingers to part from the flesh. Using a teaspoon insert ricotta mix and pull back down skin to make pockets of the ricotta. Drizzle ovenproof pan with oil and fry the drumsticks, thighs (if using) for 5 minutes until crisp and golden. Add the breasts, skin-side up with a large knob of butter, garlic and wine. Cook at top of oven for 25 minutes. Add the pomegranate seeds and cook for an extra 5 minutes.
Remove pan and place back on hob, add spinach which will wilt.
Serve the poultry selection with pan sauce and plain boiled potatoes.
## Ingredients
- Deconstructed Guinea Fowl or Chicken
- 100g ricotta cheese
- 1 small handful fresh thyme leaves
- olive oil
- butter
- 1 clove garlic, finely sliced
- 1/2 glass white wine
- pomegranate seeds
- 2 handfuls thoroughly washed baby leaf spinach
