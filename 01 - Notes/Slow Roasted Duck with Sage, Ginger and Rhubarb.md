## Method
Preheat oven 180 / gas 4. Season duck inside and out. Coarsely grate half the rhubarb and ginger. Mix in bowl with half the sage, all the garlic and onion, then stuff inside the cavity. Place on tray and roast for 1 hour. Turn down to gas 2 and cook for another 1.5 hours until crisp and tender.
**note:** will need to drain fat at least three times throughout cooking.

Allow duck to rest on a warm plate. Drain off any remaining excess fat. This can be kept for roast potatoes and such like in the fridge. Pull out the stuffing and any juices into the roasting tin and heat over a low hob. Add marsala, and de-glaze. May flame. Add stock and reduce.
Pass through seive. Remove breasts and thighs and plate up. Fry off remaining ginger and rhubarb as slices in a little oil with sage leaves. Sprinkle over the duck and spoon over the rich sauce.
Serve with a salad of hearty vegetable. Maybe Greens, wilted spinach? 
## Ingredients
- small Duck
- fresh ginger
- stick rhubarb
- fresh sage leaves
- 1/2 bulb of garlic, cloves removed and chopped in half.
- 1 red onion, finely sliced
- 1 glass marsala
- 1/4 pint vegetable or chicken stock
- olive oil
