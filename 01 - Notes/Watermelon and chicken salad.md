# Grilled Chicken, Watermelon & Avocado Salad

> A light yet satisfying summer main course for two, combining smoky grilled chicken with sweet watermelon, creamy avocado and a bright lime dressing.

- **Serves:** 2
- **Prep Time:** 15 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 15–20 minutes

---

## Ingredients

### Salad
- 2 medium chicken breasts (350–400 g)
- 500 g watermelon, cut into 2–3 cm cubes
- 1 ripe avocado, sliced
- ½ cucumber, sliced into ribbons or half-moons
- 100 g mixed salad leaves (rocket, baby spinach and little gem work well)
- 10–12 fresh mint leaves
- Small handful fresh basil leaves
- 2 tbsp toasted pumpkin seeds *(or toasted flaked almonds as an alternative)*

### Chicken Seasoning
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp garlic granules
- ½ tsp dried oregano
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper

### Lime & Honey Dressing
- 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- Juice of 1 lime
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp runny honey
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper

---

## Method

### 1. Season the chicken

Pat the chicken breasts dry and coat with the olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic granules, oregano, salt and pepper.

Heat a griddle pan, barbecue or heavy frying pan until hot. Cook for **6–8 minutes per side**, depending on thickness, until the internal temperature reaches **75°C**.

Transfer to a plate and rest for **5–10 minutes** before slicing.

---

### 2. Prepare the dressing

Whisk together:

- Olive oil
- Lime juice
- Dijon mustard
- Honey
- Salt
- Black pepper

Taste and adjust with a little more lime if you prefer a sharper dressing.

---

### 3. Toast the seeds

Toast the pumpkin seeds (or flaked almonds) in a dry frying pan for 2–3 minutes until lightly golden and fragrant.

---

### 4. Assemble the salad

Arrange the salad leaves on a large serving platter or divide between two bowls.

Top with:
- Watermelon
- Cucumber
- Avocado
- Mint leaves
- Basil leaves

Lay the sliced chicken over the salad.

Scatter over the toasted seeds.

Drizzle generously with the dressing immediately before serving.

---

## Optional Additions

- Handful of pickled red onions
- Toasted pine nuts instead of pumpkin seeds
- Burrata or mozzarella for a richer version
- Warm sourdough or ciabatta
- Boiled new potatoes for a heartier meal

---

## Notes

The sweetness of the watermelon balances the smoky chicken, while avocado provides richness without making the salad heavy. Fresh herbs and lime keep the flavours bright, making this an ideal warm-weather dinner.

**Wine pairing:** Sauvignon Blanc or dry Provençal rosé.

**Beer pairing:** Crisp pilsner or light pale ale.
