## Description
Gordon Ramsey's recipe for Cottage Pie. On stove, simple. Not for slow cooker.
## Method
Pre-heat oven 200C / Gas 6
Heat oil in large pan and brown seasoned mince. Drain. Fry onions, garlic and thyme. Add the mince and tomato puree and sprinkle with flour. Cook out the flour then add wine and worcester sauce. Reduce my half.
Add the stock, reduce heat and simmer 30-40 mins until thick and glossy.
Meanwhile cook potatoes until tender. Mash, then beat in egg yolks. Remove meat from the heat and let cool slightly. Taste for seasoning. Fork over the mash. Bake in over 30 mins.
(Optional) Grated Cheddar over the top.
## Ingredients
- Lean Beef mince 900g
- Olive oil
- Onions 450g. Finely chopped.
- 2 cloves garlic. 
- 2 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves picked and chopped
- 2 Tbsp Tomato Puree
- 25g Flour
- 200ml red wine
- 75ml Worcestershire sauce
- 1 ltr brown chicken stock
- 1 kg Desiree potatoes, peeled and halved.
- 2 egg yolks
