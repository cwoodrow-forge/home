## Description
Jamie's toad in the hole with red onion gravy and rosemary.
## Method
Mix batter ans set to one side. Use as narrow a tin as possible. Pour a scant 1cm sunflowe oil into tin and place in middle of over on highest setting! When hot add sausages and allow them to colour in oven. Take out of overn, add batter and a few sprigs of rosemary. Back into oven for at least 20 minutes (do **not** open door). Remove when golden and crispy.

For onion gravy saute onions and garlic in the butter. Add balsamic and a little stock.

Serve with mash and greens. Or baked beans.
## Ingredients
- Large butchers sausages
- Sunflower oil
- Fresh rosemary
- 2 large red onions
- 1 clove garlic, peeled and sliced
- 2 knobs butter
- 6 Tbsp balsamic
- Veg stock
#### For the batter (half if too much)
- 285ml Milk
- 115g plain flour
- 3 eggs
- pinch salt
