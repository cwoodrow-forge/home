## Description
A classic #mexican Guacamole, mashed and served in a mortar. Amazing as a dip for Tortillas, [[Nachos]], [[Chilli]] or [[Chicharron]]. 
## Method
Mash the Diced onion and green chilli in a mortar and pestle until it forms a paste.
Finely chop the coriander and add to the mortar along with the diced Avocado and salt. Mash the avocado and coriander into the onion and chilli pulp until it forms a nice rough dip.
Add squeeze of lime and stir through the chopped tomatoes and taste. Add more lime if required.
## Ingredients
- 1/2 Onion. Diced
- 1 - 2 Green Chillies. Deseeded and chopped
- 2 - 3 Ripe Avocado. Diced
- 1 Lime
- Handful Coriander. Chopped
- 1 - 2 Tomatoes. Diced
- Sea Salt
