## Description
Classic prawn cocktail flavours in a proper salad. Quick to put together, good for a sunny day.

## Method
1. Fry streaky bacon until properly crispy, drain on paper and set aside
2. In the same pan with the bacon fat, fry raw prawns for 2 minutes until pink and lightly coloured
3. While prawns cook, make the Marie Rose — mix mayo, ketchup, Worcestershire and cayenne with a squeeze of lemon to loosen
4. Build the salad: shredded iceberg, halved cherry tomatoes, sliced avocado, warm prawns, crumbled bacon
5. Dress at the table

## Ingredients
- Raw king or tiger prawns
- 4–6 rashers streaky bacon
- 1–2 ripe avocados
- ½ iceberg lettuce, shredded
- San Marzano cherry tomatoes, halved (optional)

### Marie Rose
- 2 tbsp mayonnaise
- 1 tsp ketchup
- Dash Worcestershire sauce
- Pinch cayenne
- Squeeze of lemon
