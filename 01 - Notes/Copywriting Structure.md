# Copywriting Structure

5 proven frameworks for structuring any piece of content — newsletters, tweets, YouTube scripts, sales pages. Use as a reference while writing.

---

## The Universal Structure

### Hook
Stop the scroll. Create a pattern interrupt. Make them want to keep reading.
-

### Key Points
1-3 big ideas that deliver value.
-

### Call to Action
Make them do something.
-

---

## PAS — Problem, Aggravate, Solution

### Problem
State the problem your reader is facing.
-

### Aggravate
Make it vivid. Show how it negatively affects their life.
-

### Solution
Give a concrete solution that moves them toward their desired state.
-

---

## What, Why, How

### What
State the topic.
-

### Why
Explain why it matters.
-

### How
Explain how to do it or think about it.
-

---

## APAG — Attention, Perspective, Advantage, Gamify

### Attention
Grab attention by stating the problem and its consequences.
-

### Perspective
Share your unique take. Explain why common beliefs are wrong. Use examples and social proof.
-

### Advantage
Explain the benefits of your perspective.
-

### Gamify
Give a concrete step-by-step plan to move from pain to result.
-

---

## AIDA — Awareness, Interest, Desire, Action

### Awareness
Hook in under 2 seconds.
-

### Interest
Talk about the problem and solution. Shift their beliefs from A to B.
-

### Desire
Describe the transformation, product, or idea — and its benefits.
-

### Action
Remove the last blockage. Make the next step obvious and frictionless.
-
