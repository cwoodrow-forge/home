# 5-Step Newsletter Creation Workflow

**What it does:** End-to-end newsletter workflow — from raw idea to polished final draft — using the APAG framework.

**How to use:** Run through each step in sequence. Copy and paste each prompt into Claude or ChatGPT.

---

## Step 1: Ideation

Generate strategic questions to develop your angle.

```
Ask me 5-7 strategic questions about [NEWSLETTER TOPIC] to help me develop my thoughts and explore angles I might not have considered. Focus on uncovering unique perspectives, audience pain points, and potential solutions.
```

---

## Step 2: Structured Outline (APAG)

Transform raw ideas into a structured outline.

```
You are an expert newsletter ghostwriter. Transform my raw ideas into a structured outline following the APAG framework:

1. ATTENTION: A compelling hook that highlights a problem or opportunity
2. PERSPECTIVE: My main insights organized in a logical flow that builds authority
3. ADVANTAGE: The benefits and key takeaways readers gain
4. GAMIFY: A simple, low-friction call-to-action

For each section, provide 3-5 bullet points capturing the essential ideas.

Here are my raw ideas on [TOPIC]: [Insert your notes]
```

---

## Step 3: First Draft

```
Using my outline and writing style as reference, write a first draft of my newsletter about [TOPIC]:

1. Write in my authentic voice
2. Follow the APAG framework but make it flow naturally
3. Include stories, analogies, and concrete examples
4. Keep paragraphs short for easy reading
5. Create a compelling subject line that generates curiosity
```

---

## Step 4: Review

```
Analyze this newsletter draft and provide feedback on:
1. Structure and flow
2. Engagement factors
3. Clarity of message
4. Persuasiveness
5. Areas that could be strengthened
```

---

## Step 5: Final Version

```
Create an improved version of this newsletter based on the analysis. Focus on:
1. Strengthening the opening to increase open rates
2. Adding concrete examples where needed
3. Enhancing the personal connection
4. Making the call to action more compelling
5. Maintaining my authentic voice throughout
```

---

**Final step:** Add a personal touch at the beginning or end before sending. The human moments are what make newsletters worth reading.
