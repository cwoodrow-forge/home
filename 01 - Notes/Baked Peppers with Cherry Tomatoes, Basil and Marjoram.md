## Description
Stuffed peppers with cherry tomatoes and garlic and herbs. Would probably be good with balsamic and mozzarella.
## Method
Preheat over 200 / gas 6. Cut Peppers in half and de-core. Place on oiled baking sheet and season inside. Prick the tomatoes with tip of a knife and boil for 30 - 60 seconds. Drop in cold water and pinch off the skins. Stuff the peppers with the tomatoes, herbs and sliced garlic. Drizzle with oil and bake in over for 15 minutes lightly covered with foil and 30 more minutes without. Serve with a little splash of herb vinegar.
## Ingredients
- 2 peppers (one red, one yellow)
- 20 cherry tomatoes
- small handful fresh marjoram
- small handful basil leaves
- 1 clove garlic, sliced
- extra virgin olive oil
