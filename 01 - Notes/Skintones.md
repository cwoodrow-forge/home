## Heads and faces
### Tor Garadon (Richard Grey)
1. Khorne Red over Black
2. Cadian Fleshtone - Prominent features
3. Use as a glaze to soften the transitions
4. Kislev for highlights
5. Keep using Cadian glaze to soften
For the hair:
6. Very dilute Skavenblight
7. Dabbled Daemonette Hide
For the eyes:
8. Use a base of pale grey instead of white as easier to block in with.
### Female Skintones
- Tallarn Flesh + Bright Red + tiny amount strong blue
