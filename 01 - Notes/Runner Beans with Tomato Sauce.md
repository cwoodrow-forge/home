## Description
Simple side of beans and tomato.
## Method
Blanch beans in boiling water until tender. Fry garlic in some olive oil, add tomatoes and simmer for around 15 minutes. Season then add the beans and stir. 
## Ingredients
- 200g runner beans, sliced on diagonal
- 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
- extra virgin olive oil
- 400g tin chopped tomatoes
- seasoning.
