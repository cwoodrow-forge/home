## Description
The rhubarb acts like an apple sauce for the pork #meat. Another simple and fresh dish that pairs well with roasted potatoes.
## Method
Preheat oven 220 / Gas 7. Bash up half the sage in mortar and pestle, add garlic and smash. Add olive oil then rub over the pork fillet. Allow to marinate for at least 1 hour if possible. 
Drape 5 slices of proscuitto over the fillet, wrapping it lightly. Slice the rhubarb into finger sized pieces and place in a roasting tray (no aluminium! It taints the rhubarb). Place pork and marinate over the top. Wet a sheet of baking parchment, scrunch up and tuck it over the pork and rhubarb like a blanket.
Cook for 15 minutes, remove the paper and cook a further 15 minutes until pork is cooked and ham crispy. Remove and rest for 5 minutes.
Slice at an angle. Serve with the tray rhubarb, juices and roast potatoes.
## Ingredients
- handful fresh sage
- clove garlic
- olive oil
- 1 pork fillet, trimmed
- 6 slices parma ham or prosciutto
- 6 long sticks baby rhubarb
