## Description
Warm salad of red onion and crispy pancetta with rocket, pinenuts and parmesan. Balsamic.
## Method
Peel, halve and quarter the red onions, then quarter again. Fry pancetta until crisp. Remove, add olive oil to pan then the onions, pinenuts and a handful of thyme leaves. Gentry fry for 5 minutes. return pancetta to pan and give all a toss to mix. Throw into a bowl with handfuls of rocket  and drizzle with balsamic. Serve with shavings of parmesan.
## Ingredients
- 2 Red onions
- Pancetta cubes or 8 rashers
- thyme sprigs
- pinenuts
- rocket
- balsamic
- olive oil
- parmesan
