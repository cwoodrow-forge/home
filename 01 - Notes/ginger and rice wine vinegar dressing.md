## Description
Asian inspired salad dressing. Good for picking cucumbers.
## Method
Mix together in bowl and season.
## Ingredients
- 6 Tbsp Extra Virgin olive oil
- 3 Tbsp rice wine vinegar
- 1 tsp sugar
- Thumbnail piece root ginger grated
- 1 stick lemongrass, outer leaves removed. Finely chopped
- Soy sauce
- Seasoning
