## Fabrics
### Leather
- Rhinox, scale Orange Leather, Edge Dechala Lilac
- Hull Red + Dryad Bark, Sunny Skintone (added to base mix), Sepia wash.
### Scrolls and paper
- English Uniform, Iroko, Deck Tan (Blend across all three)
- Zandri Dust, Balar Brown, Zamesi Desert
### Forgeword Track Mix
- (1) Mournfang Brown
- (2) Abaddon Black
- (2) Dryad Bark
- (3) Trollslayer Orange
- Thinned with airbrush thinners/Isopropyl. 
Mix should be 1:2:2:3 ratio before thinners.
