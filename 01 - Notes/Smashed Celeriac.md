## Description
Simple Celeriac as a side for any meat, or just a good way to use up half a one.
## Method
Slice and dice up the peeled celeriac. Add good glug of olive oil to a pot, add the celeriac, thyme and garlic with a little seasoning. Give it a shake, then fry quick for 5 minutes to get colour. Turn heat right down, add the stock or water, cover and cook for 25 minutes until tender. Smash it, or mash it, or leave a cubed. All work.
## Ingredients
- 1/2 Celeriac, peeled.
- olive oil
- handful thyme leaves picked
- 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
- 3 - 4 tablespoons stock or water
