## Description
This could be #vegetarian or a base for a #fish main. Spare fennel needs using?
## Method
Preheat oven 220 / gas 7. Slice fennel into quarters and eighths, Cook for 10 mins in boiling water. Transfer to a roasting tin. Prick the tomatoes and add to the water for 30 seconds. Transfer to cold water, pinch off skins then add tomatoes to the fennel. Add the olives, thyme and garlic. Season, drizzle olive oil, add wine and butter. Arrange everything so one layer then roast for 30 minutes. 
## Ingredients
- 1 Bulb fennel
- 12 cherry tomatoes
- pitted black olives
- small handful fresh thyme, leaves picked
- 1 clove garlic, sliced
- extra virgin olive oil
- glass white wine or vermouth
- knob of butter
