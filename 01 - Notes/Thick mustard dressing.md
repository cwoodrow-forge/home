## Description
Dijon and wholegrain mustards with vinegar and oil. Simple as it gets.
## Method
Mix together until emulsified. Season to taste.
## Ingredients
- 6 Tbsp Extra Virgin olive oil
- 2 Tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 Tbsp Wholegrain mustard
- 2 Tbsp white wine vinegar (try tarragon vinegar!), or swap for red.
- Sea salt and fresh ground black pepper
