## Schroders Personal Wealth
Specialise in **active** fund management rather than passive, where portfolio mix is regularly evaluated and adjusted based on market expertise, with goal of outperforming benchmarks. However, this comes at an increased cost.
Statistically, fund managers 'may' not have performed as well as the S&P 500 as an index fund. Stock market volatility plays a large part in this, as did the performance of the 'Magnificent 7'

### Personal Discretionary Portfolio Service (PDPS)
This is a medium risk balanced portfolio service, for medium to long-term.
**Risk**: Medium
**Return**: Short to medium where short = > 5 years and medium 5 to 10 years.

The 'balanced' portfolio is in the middle of the portfolios risk spectrum offered by Schroders.

### The risks
- Diversification risk - both not diversifying enough, but also good performances could be diluted by other stocks performing poorly (over-diversification)
- Market risk - one-off events that can cause a class of shares, a country, or even the market as a whole to lose value. Think current events, but consider recovery statistics historically.
- Inflation risk - Risk that the value of investments does not keep pace with inflationary costs for the things we buy.
- Liquidity risk - Risk can't sell investments quickly enough at the price would like, or expected.
- Interest rate risk - Risk that fixed interest investments lose out to sudden increases in interest rates by being locked in. Converse is true if not on a fixed deal if rates fall.
- Currency risk - Foreign assets devaluating due to fluctuations in exchange rates. Think dollar to sterling conversions.
- Emerging Markets risks - risks from stocks in emerging markets being more volatile or less performant than developed markets. Can also include political or social unrest, limited liquidity, currency risks, limitations on repatriation of capital. Relatively high transaction costs or fees or even government interventions. Maybe stay away from this one as a strategy.
- Stock specific risks - See lack of diversification. Example: All stocks in LBG
- Default risk - Bond issuer defaulting. Think what would happen if US defaulted on government bonds.
- Institutional risk - Bank or institution holding capital becomes insolvent.
## Services offered
The following are the different services offers by Schroders.
### Portfolio Funds
This is a passive investment option that manages funds as part of a balanced benchmark tracking portfolio.

### Ongoing Advice Service (OAS)
This is an ongoing service with access to financial advisers and services.
- Access to SPW portal to see holdings
- Investment outlooks
- Direct access to support team
- Automated ISA top-up service
### Personal Discretionary Portfolio Service (PDPS)
This is a **managed** service. SPW make all the day-to-day investment decisions on my behalf. There are (9) different risk profiles, split between growing wealth (6) or generating income (3)
