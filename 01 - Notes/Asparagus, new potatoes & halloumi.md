## Description
Roasted potatoes and Halloumi with parsley and lemon. Served with Asparagus in butter.
## Method
Pre-heat oven to gas mark 5
Cut potatoes into 2-5cm cubes and add to a roasting tin with olive oil, bashed garlic cloves and salt & pepper. Roast for 30 minutes.
Add the diced Halloumi and roast for a further 15 - 20 minutes until golden.
Meanwhile saute the Asparagus in a little butter and hot water (cover to steam)
Remove from oven and spritz with lemon and sprinkle with the chopped parsley. 
Serve with the Asparagus and Tenderstem (optional)
## Ingredients
- New potatoes or Maris Piper
- 2 - 3 garlic cloves (unpeeled) bashed
- Olive Oil
- Asparagus
- Halloumi
- Lemon Juice
- Flat Leaf Parsley (chopped)
- Salt & Pepper
