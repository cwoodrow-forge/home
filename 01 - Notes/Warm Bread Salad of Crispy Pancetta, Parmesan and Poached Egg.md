## Description
Warm salad of crispy bread, pancetta and a poached egg. 
## Method
Preheat oven to 200/Gas 6. Remove crusts from Ciabatta and tear into pieces. Place on baking tray and drizzle with olive oil, toss with garlic and seasoning. Bake for 10 minutes. Lay pancetta over the top and bake for 5 more minutes, or until pancetta is crispy.
Mix lemon juice with olive oil and season.
Toss salad leaves, pancetta and bread with dressing.
Poach eggs and serve with slivers of parmesan.
## Ingredients
- Small Ciabatta
- Extra Virgin olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, peeled and sliced
- 12 slices Pancetta, or dry-cured streaky bacon.
- Juice of a lemon
- Eggs (very fresh)
- Rocket or mixed salad leaves
- Parmesan
