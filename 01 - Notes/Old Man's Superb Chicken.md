## Description
Another roasted #poultry recipe. Not a whole chicken though.
## Method
Preheat oven 200 / Gas 6. Chop up mushrooms. Slowly fry in oil with the garlic for about 10 mins. Season and stir in chopped parsley. Allow to cool. Basically making a duxelle.
Remove the breast fillet intact. Open up each breast from the back and stuff with the mix. Place fillet over top to seal. 
Cut pastry into 2" strips and wrap around the chicken, overlapping. Brush with egg. Cook for 35 minutes.
While cooking, add mustard and white wine to a pan and reduce until evaporated the alcohol. Add the cream and simmer until thick. Serve the breasts sliced into three over the sauce with an extra drizzle of olive oil. Would probably pair with greens like beans or wilted spinach. 
## Ingredients
- Chestnut mushrooms
- olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
- handful flat-leaf parsley, chopped
- 2 chicken breasts
- ready-roll puff pastry
- 1 egg, beaten
- 2 Tbsp wholegrain mustard
- 1 glass white wine
- 140 ml double cream
