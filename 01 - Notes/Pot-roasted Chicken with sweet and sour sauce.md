## Description
#poultry An #asian inspired variation of roasted chicken. One-pot-ish. Some effort required.
## Method
Preheat oven 190 / gas 5. Season chicken inside and out and stuff cavity with the mix of parsley and ginger. Cut peppers and red onions into quarters. Place in a cold casserole pan. Add chillies, pineapple and crushed fennel seeds. Drizzle with 3 good glugs olive oil and toss everything until well coated. Place chicken on top and rub with oil. Cook in middle over at least 1.5 hours. Once cooked, drain the juices into the pan. Remove chicken to a plate with half the veg and pineapple to keep warm.
Blitz the remaining veg with sugar and balsamic. Correct or adjust the seasoning. Blend into a smooth sauce, adding boiling water to loosen if required.
Great served with stir fry noodles or boiled rice.
## Ingredients
- small whole chicken
- handful fresh parsley, roughly chopped.
- thumb sized ginger grated with skin on.
- 1 red pepper, halved and de-seeded
- 1 yellow pepper, halved and de-seeded
- 2 red onions, quartered
- 1 or 2 red chillies, snapped in half
- 1/2 pineapple, peeled, quartered and chopped. Alternatively unsweetened tinned.
- 1 Tsp fennel seeds, bashed.
- 1 Tbsp sugar
- 3 Tbsp balsamic
