## Description
Oven roasted cod, tomatoes and mozzarella. Similar to [[Baked Cod with Prawns, Avocado and cream]] 
## Method
Preheat oven to 220 / gas 7. Place cod on oiled roasting tray or earthenware dish. Drizzle with olive oil and season. Place the tomatoes and mozzarella on top of the fillets. Grate over some parmesan and add the torn basil leaves. Bake in top of oven for 15 - 20 minutes until golden.

Serve maybe with new potatoes.
## Ingredients
- 2 x 225g cod fillets. Skinned and pin boned.
- 2 handfuls of cherry tomatoes. Halved.
- Fresh basil
- 1 ball buffalo mozzarella. Torn.
- Parmesan
- Extra Virgin olive oil
