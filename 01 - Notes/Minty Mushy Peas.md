## Description
Good with fish, obviously. Or lamb.
## Method
Boil potatoes until nearly done. Add peas then after 2 minutes add the mint. After another minute, drain into colander. Leave for a minute to steam. Put everything back into pan and mash.
Add butter and season carefully.
## Ingredients
- 1 medium potato, diced.
- 500g frozen peas
- handful fresh mint
- 1 -2 knobs butter
- sea salt
- freshly ground pepper
