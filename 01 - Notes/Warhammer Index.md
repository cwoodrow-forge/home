## Shared Painting Conventions

Across the collection, these materials appear repeatedly — useful context for palette questions:

- **Bases (Legions Imperialis):** Pale Blue Grey / Model Color Sea Blue sponge / watered sand / Mig Industrial City pigments
- **Metals:** Exhaust Manifold (dark), Duraluminium (highlight), Victorian Brass (gold trim)
- **Washes:** Drakenhof Nightshade (blue/shadow), Seraphim Sepia (warm), Lahmium Medium (mix)
- **Airbrush modulation:** Zenithal pre-shade standard across most armour recipes
- **Oils:** Used on Death Guard, Legio Mortis, and Solaria for weathering and polish effects
