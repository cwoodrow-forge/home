## Description
Spicy breakfast (or tea) curried omelette

## Method
Heat light oil. Fry onion 5 m ins until starting to colour. Add chilli, gsrlic, grated ginger and nigella seeds. Sprinkle in spices and toast for couple more minutes.
Pour in eggs and agitate into a soft omelette, pulling in from sides to roll. Add optional coriander leaves.

## Ingredients
- small onion chopped
- 1 red chilli sliced
- 1 garlic clove chopped 
- teaspoon root ginger grated
- half teaspoon Nigella seeds
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- half teaspoon ground turmeric
- pinch cinammon
- 4 eggs beaten
- Coriander leaves (optional)
