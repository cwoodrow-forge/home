## Description
An #asian take on sirloin steam with greens and aromatic sauce. Healthy.
## Method
Cook Sirloin to taste and rest. Mix the garlic, ginger, chilli, soy and lime and oil like it's a [[Chimichurra]] and drizzle over the steak while it's hot.
Cook greens in boiling water until tender and drain.  Serve the steak sliced over the green and drizzle with any remaining resting juices.

(optional) Spinach makes a good alternative, wilted.
## Ingredients
- Sirloin Steaks
- 2 Pak Choi or Bok Choy.
- 4 Tbsp Soy
- 1/2 garlic clove, minced
- Thumb sized ginger, grated
- 1 chilli, deseeded and finely chopped
- Juice 1 lime
- 2 Tbsl Olive Oil
