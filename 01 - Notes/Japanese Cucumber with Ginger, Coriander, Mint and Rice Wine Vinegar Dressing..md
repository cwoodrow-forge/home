## Description
#asian inspired pickled cucumber salad. An interesting side, but also could be a salad.
## Method
Finely slice cucumbers with a mandolin along their length. Mix with torn up mint and coriander leaves. Drizzle with [[ginger and rice wine vinegar dressing]].
## Ingredients
- 3 Mini cucumbers, sliced lengthways
- Fresh mint
- Coriander
- Ginger and rice wine vinegar dressing
