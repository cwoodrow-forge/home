## Description
Hot and spicy Jerk chicken done in oven and served with rice and beans plus corn on the cob.
## Method
Pre-heat oven 220 / gas 7. Split the chicken breats lengthways through the middle and fry skin-side down until skin is crispy.
### Jerk Sauce
Put all the ingredients into a processor and blitz to a paste/sauce. Flip the chicken, pour the sauce around them abd then drizzle the skin with honey and a sprigs of rosemary or thyme. Put in top of oven for 15 minutes.
Meanwhile make the rice and the corn.
In a frying pan with a lid, heat oil and saute the spring onions. Add the rice, the cinnamon stick and stir to coat the rice. Add the stock and the beans, cover and simmer for 10 - 12 minutes until the rice has absorbed all the stock. Leave to steam.
Simmer the sweetcorn with lid on for 8 - 10 mintes while rice is resting.
Serve the rice with chicken, sauce over the top and the corn to the side.
## Ingredients
- 2 x chicken breasts, skin on.
- Runny honey
- olive oil
- sprig fresh rosemary
- sprig fresh coriander
- 2 x corn on the cob
- 120g rice
- 180ml chicken stock
- 220g black beans
- spring onions, sliced
- 1 x cinnamon stick
#### sauce
- 4 spring onions
- small handful fresh thyme, leaves picked
- 3 fresh bay leaves, centre removed
- pinch ground gloves
- pinch ground allspice
- pinch ground nutmeg
- 6 Tbsp golden rum
- 1 tbsp runny honey
- 1 scotch bonnet chilli or red chillies or dried chilli flakes
- 3 Tbsp white wine vinegar
- 2 cloves garlic
- Extra Virgin olive oil
