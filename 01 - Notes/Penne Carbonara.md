## Description
Jamie's take on classic carbonara made with penne #pasta With my own notes on alternative method.
## Method
Cook the penne until al dente.
Slowly fry the pancetta until crispy. Break up and put to one side. In a bowl whip up egg yolks, cream and half the parmesan. Toss the hot pasta with the pancetta and the egg mix using the residual heat of the pasta. Season well with lots fresh ground black pepper and add more parmesan.

#### Alternative method
Beat two whole eggs and two egg yolks with the parmesan to make a thick paste. Add to the pasta and pancetta (off heat) and mix. Use the starchy pasta water to loosen the sauce without scrambling the egg mix. Toss to emulsify. Finish with black pepper and more parmesan if needed. The parmesan and pancetta add enough salt, so no extra needed.
## Ingredients
- 4 eggs (2 whole, 2 yolks)
- Pancetta slices or cubed.
- Parmesan
- 100ml double cream (optional)
- olive oil (optional)
