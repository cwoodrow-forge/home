## CASH FLOW

Regular day-to-day and monthly spending on essentials and discretionary. 
### Essentials
These are 'essential' costs that must be covered and are broken down into following sub-categories:
- Bills
- Food
- Transport
- Debt liabilities (Mortgage, Credit Card, Borrowing)
### Discretionary
These are non-essential costs that are "nice to have" and quality-of-life. Important, but not essential to survival. Broken into sub-categories:
- Subscriptions
- Hobby/Other
- Holidays
- Entertainment
