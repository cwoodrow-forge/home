## Heat Weapon effects
### Volkite Orange
2-3 coats Windsor & newton Acrylic White
50/50 VGA Hot Orange + FX Fluor Orange (2 coats minimum)
White Ink at base
### Plasma Blue (LI)
White ink
Airbrush white ink to soften surrounds
Contrast Aetheric Blue
Windsor & Newton Titanium White to focus
White Ink edge focus
Coil tops Stegadon Green
### Meltas (Richard Grey)
Working in order from front to back. Airbrush into the vents.
1. Mephiston Red
2. Trollslayer Orange
3. Flashgitz Yellow
Paint over the exterior grills.
Magnesium.
Agrax Dunes over Victorian Brass
To create the heat bloom working from back to front.
4. Contrast Iyanden Yellow
5. Contrast Magos Purple
6. Contrast Tallasar Blue
Chrome for the extreme edges and curves.
