## Description
Posh #lamb rack, mint and vegetables.
## Method
Preheat oven 200 / gas 6. Criss-cross fat on lamb - this will help it render and become crisp.
Slice aubergines crossways into pieces and tomatoes in half. lighty brush aubergine slices with olive oil and fry both sides. remove and place on one seide of a clean roasting tray. Add the tomatoes and whole garlic cloves beside them, sprinkle with oregano and seasoning. 
Season lamb and fry in a pan until golden on all sides. Drizzle with olive oil, place skin-side up next to the vegetables and bake for 30 minutes (to pink). Rest for 5 minutes.
Bash mint in a mortar and pestle with pinch salt and sugar. Add 2 Tbsp red wine vinegar and 6 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil. Season to taste.
Cur rack into ribs and serve on warm plates with the veg and the mint sauce spooned over the lamb. 3 cutlets each (+1 for chef!)
## Ingredients
- 7-rib rack of lamb. French trimmed.
- 1 firm aubergine
- 4 ripe plum tomatoes
- extra virgin olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, skin on.
- dried oregano
- large handful fresh mint
- red wine vinegar
- pinch sugar & salt.
