## Method
Melt butter in sauce pan. Add diced leek and splash of water. Cover and cook low for 5 minutes.
Cut tips off asparagus and reserve. Finely slice stems, add to leeks along with shredded lettuce. Cook for another couple  minutes, add peas and hot stock. Season nand simmer 5 minutes.
While soup is simmering, grill asparagus tips until charred.
Blend soup until smooth, squeeze in lime or lemon. Chop herbs and stir into cream.
Serve soup drizzled with herb cream and topped with grilled asparagus.

## Ingredients
- 10g butter
- 1 leek trimmed and finely diced
- 100 asparagus
- 2 little germ lettuce, shredded
- 600g frozen peas
- 1 ltr hot vegetable stock
- squeeze lemon or lime
- single cream
- basil or mint
