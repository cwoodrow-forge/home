## William Woodcock

In Fontenay-le-Pesnel Cemetery, Tessel Calvados, France is the Commonwealth War Grave of 3457287 Fusilier William Woodcock serving with the 2/5th Battalion Lancashire Fusiliers who died 03/08/1944.

The son of William and Annie May Woodcock and the husband of Jenny Woodcock of Chester le Street

William Woodcock was born April 12th 1920 in Lancashire. In 1939 his family were living at 120 Drury Lane Chadderton, Lancashire father William born 1890 worked in the Cotton trade, mother Anne M was born 1894, sister Ethel born October 20th 1916 was an Electric Instrument Assistant and William was a Boot and Shoe repairer.

William married Jenny Embleton in 1943. Their daughter Carole was born in the same year.

William Woodcock is remembered at West Pelton on [parish book of remembrance](https://www.newmp.org.uk/memorial/book-of-remembrance-1939-45-st-paul-w112-03/)
