## Description
Simple baked new potatoes with rosemary. Be a good side for meat, especially beef or lamb.
## Method
Preheat oven 220 / gas 7.
Wash and parboil potatoes until almost tender. Drain and drizzle with a little oilive oil then roll in a tablespoon of Maldon sea salt, black pepper and the rosemary.
Roast for 25 minutes.
## Ingredients
- Jersey Royal or new potatoes
- olive oil
- Maldon sea salt
- black pepper
- Sprig rosemary, leaves picked, chopped and bashed.
