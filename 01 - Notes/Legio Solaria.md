Legio Solaria, cognomen the Imperial Hunters.
## Titans
### Green armour
1. Prime black
2. Airbrush Caliban Green
3. Sponge Irati Green
4. Sponge VGA Goblin Green
5. Sponge Dead Flesh
6. Sponge VMC White
7. Gloss Varnish (important to do before oils)
8. Oil Viridian Hue (Polish out when dry)
9. Re-Varnish
If necessary polish with Tamiya compound to remove any unwanted textures.
### Trim
- Black
- Leadbelcher + black (2 coats)
- Wash / Glaze Sepia, Smoke, Blue ink
