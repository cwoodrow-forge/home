Simply add ready-to-wok Udon Noodles to the [[Asian Peanut Dressing]] and you have a ready made summer noodle dish.

## Ingredients
- Ready to Wok Udon Noodles
- Sliced spring onions
- Sliced red chillies (optional)
- Chopped fresh Coriander (optional)

## Method
Create the Asian Peanut Dressing as linked in a pan. Warm noodles in hot water until ready, drain and add to dressing. Top with spring onions and optional chillies or coriander.
