## Description
Penne pasta with cherry tomatoes, garlic and basil sauce. From Gordon Ramsey.
## Method
In a frying pan melt butter until foamy then add garlic for a few seconds and the halved cherry tomatoes, salt and pepper. Cover and simmer on low until the tomatoes break down into a sauce.
Meanwhile cook the penne. When al dente add the pasta to the tomatoes and loosen sauce with some of the starchy pasta water.
Tear the basil and add to the sauce before serving with fresh Parmesan.
## Ingredients
- Punnet of cherry tomatoes
- 2 - 3 cloves of garlic. Crushed.
- Penne pasta
- Fresh Basil
- Fresh Parmesan
