## Description
US style #breakfast pancakes.
## Method
Separate the eggs. Add flour, baking powder and milk to the yolks and mix to a smooth batter.
Whisk the whites with salt to stiff peaks.
Fold into the batter.
Drop onto non-stick pan and when first side is done, add topping of choice and flip to cook other side.
#### Topping choices
- Blueberries
- Crispy bacon or pancetta
- Banana
- Stewed apple
- anything you can think of.
Serve with Maple syrup, butter, creme fraiche
## Ingredients
- 3 large eggs
- 115g plain flour
- 1 heaped tsp baking powder
- 140ml milk
- pinch salt
