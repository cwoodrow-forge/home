## Description
Sweet breakfast bread.
## Method
Remove the cherry pips. Sprinkle with the vanilla sugar and set aside. Make a focaccia dough and prove . After bulk brove, transfer to baking tin and make dimples into dough with oiled fingers. Divide the cherries and push into the dough. Sprinkle  remaining sugar over vread and dot with the butter. Leave to double in size and bake in oven 180 / gas 4 for 25 - 30 minutes. 
Best served warm at #breakfast with coffee. or nice as a #desert with ice cream on top.
## Ingredients
- Basic focaccia bread recipe
- ripe cherries
- 4 heaped tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 115g butter
