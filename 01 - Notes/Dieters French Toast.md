## Description
Low fat mix of apple compote and a lighter French toast. For breakfast or desert.

## Method
Make compote: Diced peeled apples, water and lemon juice in a saucepan. Simmer until softened. Sprinkle in cinammon and pour over maple syrup.

Break eggs into a bowl with milk and sugar and cinammon. whisk until thoroughly combined into a batter.
Press bread into the batter until soaked through. Skake excess and immediately fry. 2 minutes each side until browned.
Serve with the apple compote , a dollop of Greek Yoghurt or creme fraiche and a handful of blackberries or spare blueberries.
## Ingredients
- 2 large eggs
- 200ml whole milk
- 1 tsp sugar
- half teaspoon cinnamon
- 4 thick slices bread
- 2 large bramley apples. peeled and diced
- squeeze lemon juice
- half teaspoon cinnamon
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- berries (optional)
- Low fat greek yoghurt or creme fraiche (optional)
