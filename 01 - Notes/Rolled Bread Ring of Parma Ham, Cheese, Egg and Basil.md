## Description
A stuffed wreath that would make a good picnic, or sharing bread for a buffet.
## Method
Make basic bread dough. Go through both bulk prove and stretching into a long rectangle. Along the middle of the bread lay out the parma ham, eggs, cheese and basil (olives and sun dried tomatoes optional). Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil and season. Pull the dough from either side into a long sausage, pinch together then bring the ends together to create a ring and pinch together. Transfer to a floured baking tin, dust with flour and allow to prove for 15 minutes. Bake at 180 / gas 4  for 35 minutes. 
## Ingredients
- [[basic bread mix]]
- 10 slices parma ham
- 6 boiled eggs, sliced
- grated cheese, can be a mix of cheddar, goats cheese, fontina, parmesan. Anything leftover will do.
- handful fresh basil
- sun-dried tomatoes (optional)
- black olives (optional)
- extra virgin olive oil
