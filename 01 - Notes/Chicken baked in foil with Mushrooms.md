## Description
Chicken breasts baken in a bag with mushrooms, white wine and herbs. See also [[Chicken and cannellini beans and leeks]].
## Method
Make a large foil envelope, open at one end. Preheat over to 220 / gas 7. Mix everything in a bown and add to the bag, seal. Carefully slide onto a roasting tray and place over a hob for 1 minute to get the cooking going. Bake in over for 25 minutes. Open at table and serve.
## Ingredients
- 2 x chicken breasts
- 1 handful dried porcini
- Mixed mushrooms, torn up
- 1 large glass white wine
- 3 knobs butter
- Handful fresh thyme
- 2 cloves garlic, sliced.
