## Description
A sausage breakfast muffin with fried egg and mice patties. Bit like a McMuffin. The patties are optional.

## Method
Skin sausages and shape into a single round patty. Alternatively create turkey or chicken aptties mixing mince, finely diced bacon (without the fat), sage and tomato puree.
Griddle patties on griddle like a smash burger. In fact could treat same. 
Toast the muffin halves, fry the eggs.
Build muffin, patty, cheese (melted), egg. Leave off top muffin for rduced calories
## Ingredients
- 4 Butchers Sausages
- Slices reduced fat cheese
- 4 Eggs
- 4 Muffins
