## Description
Based on a #pasta dish by US chef Mario Bartelli (two minute squid). Can also be thinned with stock to make into a #soup, or used as a #sauce over fish. Highly versatile.
## Method
In a large pot, slowly fry onion and garlic in a little olive oil for 1 minute. Add the farro, olives and tomatoes and simmer for 30 minutes. Add the mussels and clams and shake around until they open (may need to cover with a lid). Then add the squid and simmer for 1 minute. Remove the meat from the shells (Note: I would cook them separately and add to avoid the mess).
Cook the pasta until al dente, season the sauce then toss in the parsley and extra virgin olive oil until glossy. Toss the pasta in the sauce and serve.
## Ingredients
- 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
- 1 cloves garlic, finely sliced
- 30g [[farro]]
- handful black olives
- 1 x tin tomatoes
- 200g mussels, cleaned
- 200g clams, cleaned
- 155g squid, sliced
- 6 sheets fresh lasagne (or dried)
- chopped fresh parsely
- extra virgin olive oil
