## Description
A simple #pasta that makes most of really good cherry tomatoes and fresh marjoram from the garden.
## Method
Boil pasta until al-dente. Halve tomatoes and put into bown with herbs, oil, garlic and vinegar. Smush together to work dressing into the tomatoes. Drain the pasta and add to the herby tomato mix.
## Ingredients
- Spaghetti (or linguini)
- Mixed ripe cherry tomatoes
- marjoram or basil leaves
- 1 clove garlic (finely sliced)
- Extra Virgin olive oil
- 1 Tbsp red or white wine vinegar

Optional: Add black olives
