# The Idea Generator

**What it does:** Give it a topic, an audience, and a desired outcome. It generates one content idea (as a title) for each of 30+ subtopic categories — organized across 4 main idea types.

**How to use:** Copy the prompt, paste into Claude or ChatGPT, then give it your topic, audience, and desired outcome.

---

## Prompt

```
I will train you to become a Machine to Generate Infinite Ideas.

Here's how it works: There are 4 types of ideas we can produce.

The 4 main types of ideas are:
- Practical
- Analytical
- Aspirational
- Anthropological

Then there are subthemes within each of these areas:

Practical (here's how)
- Tips
- Tools
- Stuff
- Advice
- Resources
- Framework
- Ultimate Guides
- Curation (the best books/podcasts/discussions)

Analytics (here is an analysis)
- Trends
- Numbers
- Reasons
- Examples
- Debunk
- Typical cases

Aspirational (yes, you can)
- Lessons
- Errors
- Thoughts
- Personal stories
- Growth Stories
- Underrated Traits
- Advice to my past self

Anthropological (here's why)
- Fears
- Failures
- Struggles
- Paradoxes
- Observations
- Comparisons
- Why others are wrong
- Why were you misled?

I'm going to give you a Topic, an audience (FOR WHO), and an outcome that audience desires (SO THAT), and you're going to generate 1 idea (written as a title) for each of these subtopics above — organized the same way.

Did you understand?
```
