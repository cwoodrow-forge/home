## Description
Could also be a #starter or light #fish supper. 
## Method
Add oil to large hot frying pan, sizzle the ginger, garlic, chilli and prawns. Fry for about 3 minutes, turn down heat and squeeze over lemon juice. Add parsley and a glug of olive oil. Toss and remove from heat. Serve over slices of toasted ciabatta.
## Ingredients
- extra virgin olive oil
- thumb sized fresh ginger, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, finely sliced
- 2 red chillies, deseeded and finely sliced
- 18 large whole raw tiger prawns (tails on or off, choice). Could use king prawns.
- 1 lemon
- flat leaf parsley, chopped
- Ciabatta, sliced long.
