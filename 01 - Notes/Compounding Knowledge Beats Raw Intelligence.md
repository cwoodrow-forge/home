# Compounding Knowledge Beats Raw Intelligence

## Big Idea
The person who reads and connects ideas consistently over 5 years will outthink someone twice as "smart" who doesn't. Knowledge compounds — each new idea connects to existing ones, creating an exponentially larger network than the sum of its parts. A second brain isn't a productivity tool — it's a compounding machine.

### Trigger Questions
- How does this note fit into what I already know (in my other notes)?
- How can this note explain another concept already in my system?
- What idea contradicts this idea?
- Why am I attracted to this idea (apart from confirmation bias)?
- If we change the author's perspective, does this note evolve?
- What are the applications I will have of this concept?
