Is this "is a" main note type?
Examples: #palette #guide #reference #project #recipe  
These should go into the front matter as a note property with 'type' of tag. They are not immutable, but less likely to change.

What is the status?
#done #unread #read #todo
These also go into the front matter, but they can change. For example, changing from 'todo' to 'done', or 'unread' to 'read'.

Is it an "about" note?
Examples: #epic #heresy #mexican #money 
These tags can also got into the tag property front matter, or as section headings.

In-line Tags.
These can be general tags to help search or tracks specific topics.
Example: #ideas #question
